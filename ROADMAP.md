2. Compare symbolic exactness classes against numerically generated residual fields.
3. Strengthen the bridge doc to real plasma settings without overselling current reach.

## Longer Term

1. Determine whether the variable-resistivity obstructions admit useful reduced models.
2. Build a clearer link to variable-coefficient parabolic PDE structure and resistive-induction theory.
