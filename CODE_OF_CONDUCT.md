# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus feedback on code, methods, and evidence.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements with clarity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Intimidation or repeated hostile interactions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.
