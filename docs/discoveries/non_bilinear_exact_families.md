# Non-Bilinear Exact Families

The earlier discovery memo emphasized the power-family sample `alpha = r^n`, `beta = r*theta`.

The stronger statement supported by the new symbolic pass is broader:

## Exact family A

Under constant `eta`, every smooth radial family

```text
alpha = f(r), beta = r*theta
```

has zero residual in the cylindrical closure calculation used here.

That means the power-family result was a special case of a larger exact class.

## Exact family B

Under the same constant-resistivity setting,

```text
alpha = f(r), beta = z
```

is also exact.

## Why this matters

These statements shift the repo from "we found a new power family" to "we have identified broad radial exact classes." That is a stronger and cleaner mathematical contribution for this program than the earlier memo alone.
